About Seattle University

Seattle University is a Jesuit Catholic university located on a beautiful 50-acre campus in the heart of Seattle. Our diverse and passionate student body is made up of more than 7,400 students representing all 50 states and more than 60 countries.

As a leading independent university in the Northwest, Seattle University has been shaping leaders and making an impact in our communities and beyond since 1891. Through our mission of educating leaders for a just and humane world, we prepare our students for lives of meaning and purpose.
